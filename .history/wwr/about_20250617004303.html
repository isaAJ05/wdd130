Rafting Travel

At Rafting Travel, our mission is to connect people with nature through thrilling and safe white water rafting experiences.
We believe that adventure brings people together and that the river has something to teach us all.

Our motto is: "Ride the river. Live the moment."

History

Founded in 2010, Rafting Travel began as a small group of passionate guides who wanted to share the joy of river rafting with others. What started as weekend trips with friends quickly grew into a trusted adventure company offering safe and unforgettable rafting tours for all experience levels.

Over the years, we have expanded our operations across multiple rivers and built a team of certified, experienced guides. We continue to uphold our commitment to environmental stewardship, customer satisfaction, and memorable outdoor experiences.
